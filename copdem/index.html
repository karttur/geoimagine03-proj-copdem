Copernicus DEM processing

This second part of the blog on global Digital Elevation Models (DEMs) detaiels the processing of the Copernicus DEM (CopDEM) for the Northern Hemisphere using the EASE 2 Grid North projection. To access CopDEM and understand some of the processing principles you might want to consult part 1 on Global DEMs.
